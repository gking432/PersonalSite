## Design & Technical Specifications

### Visual Style
- **Clean, minimal, professional**
- Not overly "techy" but modern
- White/light gray background
- Accent color: Something bold but professional (navy blue, deep teal, or similar)
- High-quality images/screenshots
- Plenty of white space

### Typography
- Sans-serif, modern, readable
- Clear hierarchy (H1, H2, body text)
- No excessive bolding or formatting

### Interaction Elements
- Smooth scrolling
- Hover states on project cards
- Simple animations (fade-ins, nothing distracting)
- Fast load times

### Mobile Responsiveness
- Must look great on phone/tablet
- Touch-friendly buttons
- Simplified navigation on mobile

### Technical Stack Recommendation
- **Framework**: React (since you're comfortable with it + can showcase your skills)
- **Hosting**: Vercel or Netlify (free, fast, easy deploys)
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe (for speaking events)
- **Email Integration**: EmailJS or Formspree (for contact form)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics
- **CMS for Blog** (optional): If you want to host writing on-site instead of just linking to Substack
